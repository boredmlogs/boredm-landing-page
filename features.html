What sets BoreDM apart?

Features

A trusted platform dedicated to simplifying borings for everyone.
From logging to reports, we offer everything.

Custom Boring Logs

Design your own log templates with the exact fields, symbols, and styles your team needs. Maintain consistency across projects without wrestling with manual formatting every time

Field Collection App

Capture soil data on your phone or tablet—online or offline—and sync automatically when you get back to Wi-Fi. Eliminate paper forms and transcription errors by sending field notes straight into your main project database.

Civil 3D connection

Push your bore logs and cross-sections directly into AutoCAD Civil 3D as georeferenced objects. Save hours on drafting and ensure your designs always match the latest subsurface data.

GINT, DIGGS & AGS Support

Import and export borehole records in every major industry format without losing detail. Keep working with legacy datasets or share files seamlessly with partners using GINT, DIGGS, or AGS.

PDF + CAD Cross Sections

Generate to-scale, fully editable cross sections as PDF for clients and DWG for engineers. Deliver polished, ready-to-use visuals that cut out manual redraws and shorten review cycles.

ASTM Lab testing

Link your soil lab results directly to bore logs in line with ASTM standards. Quickly flag any out-of-spec samples and track test history in one clear interface.

How is my data protected?

Security features

At BoreDM, security is engineered into every layer of our platform. We enforce strict encryption and access controls to keep your subsurface and lab data safe.

SOC 2 Type II & ISO 27001 Compliance

We undergo annual, independent audits to validate our controls against SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 standards. Your data handling—from access to storage—is governed by proven, best-practice security policies.

Data Encryption

All data in transit is protected by TLS 1.2+ and encrypted at rest with AES-256; keys are managed in a dedicated, hardened system. This ensures that only authorized services can decrypt your information—ever.
