Capabilities

Powerful, intuitive features

BoreDM is committed to supporting every user on our platform. We develop personal relationships with our user community, taking weekend phone calls from small businesses and building out dedicated teams for enterprise organizations.

Detailed GIS mapping

View boreholes, test pits, CPTs, and other exploration points on high-resolution basemaps, and see your job sites from street view where possible.

Customizable boring logs

Highlight the most critical information with fully customizable logs. Control editing permissions for your library’s templates to ensure organization-wide consistency.

Mobile field collection

Use our industry-leading mobile app to log soil and rock samples in real time on your smart device, even without a signal. Once you’re back online, entries sync seamlessly. Be on the lookout for voice-based logging in early 2026.

Embedded lab integration

Assign laboratory testing for your project’s samples, then run the tests directly within the BoreDM platform. Track equipment and capacity, generate professional reports, and autofill results directly back into your boring logs.

AI log scanner & digitizer

Sitting on boxes of old borings? Our proprietary AI tool can digitize handwritten and PDF records alike. Our technology will pulls depths, layers, and notes into review‑ready records in minutes. No manual typing, even for decades‑old logs.

Editable cross‑section exports

BoreDM’s powerful visualization modules enable you to quickly produce to‑scale cross‑sections as editable PDFs or DWGs — ready for client review or engineering edits. Say goodbye to the days of manual redraws.

Seamless format compatibility

BoreDM is compatible with your existing workflows. Drop any gINT, DIGGS, or AGS files into BoreDM, and we’ll keep all the original file attributes remain intact. When you're ready, export back to the same formats.

Comprehensive data safeguards

With end‑to‑end encryption, role‑based permissions, and real‑time auditing, BoreDM ensures your subsurface data is always protected.

SOC 2 Type II & AZRAMP Compliance

We undergo yearly independent audits to ensure our controls meet SOC 2 Type II and AZRAMP standards. At BoreDM, we take digital protection seriously: from user access through data storage, every process follows industry‑leading security policies.

Encryption & integrity

Every connection to BoreDM is secured with TLS 1.2+ and all stored records are encrypted at rest with managed keys. Automated backups and integrity checks safeguard your logs and lab results from loss or tampering, and enable recovery at the click of a button.
