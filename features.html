Interactive site maps

Visualize boreholes, test pits, CPTs, and more on dynamic basemaps. Explore sites from multiple perspectives, and integrate with GIS platforms for advanced spatial analysis.

Customizable boring logs

Highlight the most critical information with fully customizable logs. Control editing permissions for your library’s templates to ensure organization-wide consistency.

Mobile field collection

Use our industry-leading mobile app to log soil and rock samples in real time on your smart device, even without a signal. Once you’re back online, entries sync seamlessly. Be on the lookout for voice-based logging in early 2026.

Embedded lab integration

Assign laboratory testing for your project’s samples, then run the tests directly within the BoreDM platform. Track equipment and capacity, generate professional reports, and autofill results directly back into your boring logs.

AI log scanner & digitizer

Sitting on boxes of old borings? Our AI technology pulls depths, layers, and notes off of paper and PDFs in minutes. No manual typing, even for decades‑old logs.

Editable cross‑section exports

BoreDM’s powerful visualization modules enable you to quickly produce to‑scale cross‑sections as editable PDFs or DWGs — ready for client review or engineering edits. Say goodbye to the days of manual redraws.

Advanced import & export

Bring your historical data with you — BoreDM supports DIGGS, AGS, gINT, and other industry-standard formats. Import seamlessly, manage your projects in one place, and export back.

Environmental analytes

Automatically check lab results against regulatory standards. BoreDM flags exceedances in real time and highlights them directly in your logs for clear, consistent reporting.
