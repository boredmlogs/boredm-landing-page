Product

BoreDM offers two subscription types: Logs and Labs

Logs serves those out in the field, enabling our full suite of borehole capabilities. Upgrade to Logs Pro for access to premium features like 3D visualization and AI borelog scanning.

Labs works for those in the lab, enabling users to track test assignments and efficiently enter data. Upgrade to Labs Pro for access to premium features like database querying and over 20 advanced ASTM tests.

Just need to log your boreholes? Go for Logs. Just need to run tests? Go for Labs. Want to utilize the full power of the modern standard for subsurface data, integrating from field to findings? Go for Logs and Labs.

Though Logs and Labs may be purchased separately, nearly all of our current customers use both.
