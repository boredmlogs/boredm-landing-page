Breakthroughs

Built by software engineers, for geotechs

We know the pain of software tools built by civil engineers. BoreDM's founder, Louis, is a software developer who encountered gINT while working in operations for a tunneling company. Though he was frustrated and surprised by the outdated user interface of gINT, he was even more struck by the product's lack of built-in data management capabilities.

Subsequently, Louis consulted extensively with engineers across the globe to build BoreDM, short for Boring Data Management. With a team of experienced software developers, data experts, and geoprofessionals from around the world, Louis built a product which drives efficiency and reduces risk, while helping engineers build high-quality subsurface data assets.
