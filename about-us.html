Built to transform subsurface work

BoreDM is designed for the realities of subsurface work — logging in the field, running lab tests, generating cross-sections, and producing clean reports. We replace slow, fragmented tools with a single platform that’s remarkably intuitive and grounded in industry standards.

By structuring subsurface data from the start, BoreDM helps teams reduce manual errors, streamline analysis, and easily carry information across projects. These aims, though absolutely essential to our work, are not often reflected in subsurface software.

We know the pain of clunky borehole products all too well: BoreDM was started by a software developer who encountered gINT while working for a tunneling company. Frustrated and surprised by the experience of using gINT, Louis became curious and started cold-calling engineers, who ultimately challenged him to build them something better.

Subsequently, Louis and BoreDM have consulted deeply with engineers across the globe to build and maintain a product that saves time and money, while helping engineers build high-quality geotechnical and environmental data assets.
