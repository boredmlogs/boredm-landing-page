Built to Transform Subsurface Work

BoreDM is designed for the realities of geotechnical work — logging in the field, running lab tests, generating cross-sections, and producing clean reports. We replace slow, fragmented tools with a single platform that’s intuitive to use and grounded in geotechnical standards.

By structuring subsurface data from the start, BoreDM helps teams reduce manual errors, streamline analysis, and carry information across projects. Our aim is simple: better workflows, better decisions, and a clearer picture of what’s happening below the surface.

Ready‑to‑Use Integrations

Plug BoreDM into your existing infrastructure in seconds. A single sync connects raw field readings — data loggers, penetrometers, sensors — directly into your current database, so you can validate and analyze in real time without any manual steps.

On desktop, BoreDM slips right into the tools you already use: logs and cross‑sections appear natively in Civil 3D and ArcGIS, and your subsurface data stays accessible through our REST API and standard AGS/GINT formats.
